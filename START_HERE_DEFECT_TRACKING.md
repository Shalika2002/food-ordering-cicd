   - Makes you sound knowledgeable!

3. **Show Confidence**:
   - Practice until you're comfortable
   - Know your material inside out
   - Smile and make eye contact

   - Read Q&A section in viva script
   - Think of variations
   - "Good question! Let me explain..."
